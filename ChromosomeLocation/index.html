Class: ChromosomeLocation

A Location on a chromosome defined by a species and chromosome name.

URI: GA4GHVRSDefinitions:ChromosomeLocation

Slots

Name Range Cardinality Description Info
_id CURIE 0..1 Location Id. MUST be unique within document. .
type NONE 1..1 MUST be "ChromosomeLocation" .
species_id CURIE 1..1 CURIE representing a species from the NCBI species taxonomy. Default: "taxonomy:9606" (human) .
chr NONE 1..1 The symbolic chromosome name. For humans, For humans, chromosome names MUST be one of 1..22, X, Y (case-sensitive) .
interval CytobandInterval 1..1 The chromosome region defined by a CytobandInterval .
